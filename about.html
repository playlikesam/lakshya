Project Focus

Our project focuses on blended learning as a solution to overcome inadequate infrastructure. Blended learning combines traditional face-to-face instruction with online learning resources to provide a more flexible and effective learning experience. We aim to explore how blended learning can enhance education accessibility and quality, particularly in environments with limited resources.
